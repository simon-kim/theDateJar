About The Date Jar

The Date Jar is a simple application designed to spice up a relationship when things feel like they're slowing down or not being spontaneous enough. It forces couples to make time for each other in this busy world we live in and also helps people explore new things and get to know each other better. It is currently optimized for use in areas around Southern California.

Rules

The rules are simple. You pick a category and do the date given to you. To keep things interesting, you CAN NOT repick a date.

Already went on that date before? Tough luck, do it again! Don't like what was given to you? Suck it up and explore new things! Feel like the date is too difficult to plan/arrange? Sounds like an excuse, and excuses aren't allowed here.

Like Shia Lebeouf would say, just DO it. Don't let your dreams be dreams. Simple enough? Got it? Good.

Oh, and of course, have fun!
